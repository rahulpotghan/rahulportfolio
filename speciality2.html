ABOUT
ME

I am a creative UX and UI designer with great business and design background. I have helped many startups and businesses by improving their products. I specialise in product development, UI design, UX design, interactive design, graphic design, and business development.

Follow me on Twitter or catch up with my latest work on Dribbble.

I’M SPECIALISED IN

Creating Concepts & Visualizing Ideas

Every great design starts with an idea/concept. I make sure fundamental parts are well defined and working. The best way to present an idea is to visualise it as visualisation helps users understand the story that a business wants to tell them.

Service Blueprint

I am big fan of designing the customer journey as well as all of the interactions and touchpoints that make up and support that journey and work on to unpack areas of improvements and problems for the customers. It is all about capturing right emotions of users and understanding the underlying service.

Product Design & PRD's

Building products is my passion. It’s more than just design. It’s about understanding the company and its clients. It’s about complex processes and journey to achieve success. User Research, Feature Research, Data driven approach to decision making without first identifying the metrics that matter most.

METRICS DRIVEN

We live and work today in a world that is increasingly data-driven, however we cannot successfully adopt a data driven approach to decision making without first identifying the metrics that matter most.

UX ENGINEERING

I come from a Software Engineering background with experience in SAAS based and Mobile Application. Design patterns, Js frameowrks and front-end are friends to me.

 IMPROVING UX

When we take the time to craft a more user-centric experience around the things we create, we end up with a much higher quality end result, and a much happier user as well.
