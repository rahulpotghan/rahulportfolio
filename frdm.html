FRDM

Insight into the complex web

About the project

Cloud based Software as a Service (SaaS) platform FRDM and Footprints, FRDM (Forced labor Risk Determination & Mitigation) is a revolutionary software product that gives profound insight into the complex web of global business-to-business (B2B) commerce and locates specific hot spots of risk in a supply chain for any industry. I was involved in entire SDLC and also transformed product requirement to technical requirements.

My role

Lead the Product User Experience team through customer behavior and in-­‐depth analysis for performance indicator and impact by doing quantitative and quality analysis. Lead the innovation team to build out a scalable, high performance, reusable platform delivered through cloud. Product Management: Created user stories, use cases detail requirements, evaluated user stories and associated story points. Plan user acceptance and A/B Testing. Generated report and recommendations document by analyzing weekly data and conduct JAD Sessions. Collaborated and interacted with cross-­‐functional team to set up KPI interactive Metrics dashboard.

Research and Goals:

At the start of the project I did extensive research about logistics and the way people interact with devices to schedule delivery, send packets and pain points int he entire journey of sending packages though various means. With that, I created a mobile strategy for the Tranzip. Once my reserach on user was completed the idea was to prototype new features and then bring users in to test them before committing to development effort. One of the initial challenges was ensuring that the findings themselves would be verifiable both to the team itself and to other stakeholders. Unlike quantitative data, where the numbers and graphs can be readily reproduced and shared with others in an email, the outcomes of qualitative research as notes taken based on observation could be open to interpretation. So it was critical that the process would be accountable.
